Visit Port Elizabeth/Gqeberha

Enjoy visiting Kragga Kamma Game Park, exploring Sardinia Bay Beach,
or immersing yourself in the wildlife at Addo Elephant National Park.

Top three activities to do in Port Elizabeth

The Kragga Kamma Game Park is a small reserve within the Nelson Mandela Bay Municipality in the Eastern Cape, South Africa.

Sardinia Bay Beach is a popular beach that is famous for its picnic facilities and is regarded as one of the best walking beaches with miles of unspoilt coastline.

Addo Elephant National Park (AENP) is a diverse wildlife conservation park situated close to Gqeberha in South Africa and is one of the country's 20 national parks.
